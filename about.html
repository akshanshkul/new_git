ABOUT US

Who we are?

Sidmus is an ed-tech startup started with the aim of making careers. The company is recently founded in May 2022. Sidmus edtech is offering a wide choice of management and technical programs from reputed universities and institutes.

Sidmus edtech is helping universities and institutes in developing management programs that benefit the working professionals and the students who want to grow in their careers with distance learning in management and technical programs.


Our Mission

The mission of SIDMUS Edtech is to increase the students engagement in online education to improve the outcomes and enhanced individualized education.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide the better education to all the needful students and professionals which helps them in making their career.
