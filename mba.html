MBA - GLA UNIVERSITY, MATHURA

Overview

The entire Online MBA learning experience, interaction with faculty members and peers, student activities, case studies, project work, teaching aids are set in place to ensure that the education you receive is not just theoretical but also involves experiential learning. So, your knowledge of general management, business, and your chosen specializations, which could be Marketing, Finance, Human Resource, Information Technology, Business Analytics, Operations, Retail etc., will get enriched to radically change your perspective, both personally and professionally in order to better understand, analyse and handle these complex business situations.


This Online MBA Program of GLA University, nurtures leaders to make them capable of making a difference in the corporate and service sectors. By integrating technology with conventional teaching methods, enables the students to acquire well-developed understanding of management practices, knowledge, and skills necessary to grapple with the complexities of the modern business propositions. This program provides specialized knowledge, multiple skills such as critical analysis, presentation, public speaking, interpersonal communication, leadership, and attitude along with multi-tasking ability to professionals who aspire to become organizational managers in today's global business environment. BBA is one degree that serves as a ticket to a management career.

Name of Program : MBA


Duration : 2 Years


Specialization : Marketing, Human Resource, Financial Management, International Business Management, Operations Management, Banking and Financial Services, Business Analytics, Supply Chain Management
