BBA - GLA UNIVERSITY, MATHURA

Overview

This BBA program teaches several basic concepts of business administration and is a great program for anyone looking to work in the areas of Finance, Sales, Marketing, Human Resources, and Management. This program helps to develop managerial approaches from the beginning of the program and is a perfect fit to match the demand of various industries for managerial roles. This program is well adapted to train students as per the industry requirements. The combination of practical and theoretical knowledge provided in a BBA course helps increase the value of a student in the market. Well-established companies and startups stay on the lookout for our BBA students as they are suited to carry out a range of responsibilities in the organization. On completion of this program, one can easily find a lucrative entry-level job.


This BBA program offers an opportunity to acquire professional skills in the early stage of your career as a management professional. Those who pursue an MBA after BBA, there is no end to excellent job opportunities. Even studying BBA is like making lesser investments and enjoying more returns in the future in the form of an attractive salary. This degree teaches you to be a strong, confident person who is capable of handling teams and running a whole company. You are taught to be a team leader and a good team player as well. You are also taught to be a good public speaker as you have to give a lot of presentations during your BBA education. By the end of your degree, you will be a more developed and wholesome person. BBA is one degree that serves as a ticket to a management career.


Highlights

Wide Knowledge Base: This BBA program is designed by industry experts and imparts deep knowledge regarding all the aspects of running a company. It is a combination of theory, practical knowledge application, and personality development.


Personality Development: This is what sets our BBA apart from other graduate programs. In BBA, you are also taught to develop your personality. You are taught public speaking, leadership, soft skills and more during the course, BBA prepares you well.


Career Opportunities: Just after your graduation, you can join the professional world as an intern manager or in other administrative positions. You also get a handsome starting salary and other benefits packages.


Professional Degree: BBA is considered to be the best bachelor’s professional degree. There are other management degrees as well, but BBA is rated better by industry experts and students alike.


Networking Opportunities: All your fellow graduates will be working in various industries. Therefore, this becomes a strong network of professionals. You can find work, or offer work in this network. A strong network is a huge advantage.


Updated knowledge & Skills: This BBA course is a well-designed course and is also updated according to the latest trends and developments in the industry, giving students a distinct advantage.
