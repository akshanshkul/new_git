B.COM (HONS.) - GLA UNIVERSITY, MATHURA

Overview

The course curriculum includes subjects of finance & accountancy as well as business management to provide comprehensive knowledge of commerce which will help in laying a strong foundation in understanding the nuances of various subjects. This will strengthen the theoretical knowledge in addition to developing strong communication, problem-solving, critical thinking, decision-making, and strategic skills. Armed with such knowledge and skills, you will be better prepared at your first job in the existing fast-paced business world.


With an innovative curriculum design and content, the B. Com. (Hons.) program empowers students to conveniently adapt to an ever changing and dynamic business environment. The program enables students to have good fundamental knowledge of Accounting, Economics, Taxation, Statistics, Auditing, Cost Accounting, and Finance and in addition the management subjects. After the successful completion of course, the students have career prospects in the field of chartered accountancy, financial analysts, financial management etc.


Highlights

Holistic education: Holistic education is imparted for all-round development of the students. Utmost priority is given for improving the analytical and communication skills of the students.


High employment: Students of B. Com can certainly expect high employment opportunities as today every business needs a commerce specialist or an accountant to run the business successfully.


Mentoring: Each faculty has been assigned a set of students for mentoring, in order to provide counselling and guidance.


Vast Career Opportunities: This B.Com.(Hons.) program builds competence and confidence among students to pursue and complete, professional programs such CA, CFA, ICWA, ACS etc.


Eligibility

Name of Program : B.com(Hons.)


Duration : 3 Years


Specialization : Auditing, Stock Market Operations, Laws, Behaviourial Finance, Banking and Insurance, Business Analytics, Accounting and Finance, International Business


Eligibility Details : Passed 10+2 th examination with at least 50% marks in any stream


Relaxation : 5% Relaxation in the minimum eligibility is provided to SC/ST/Other specified Category Students.
